# Participant FAQ

## Is Streamlit mandatory?
Yes. Final demos must run through a Streamlit prototype.

## Can we build a dashboard instead of a search catalog?
Yes. Dashboard, catalog, and hybrid solutions are all valid.

## Do we have to use crawler code?
No. Crawler usage is optional. Baseline CSV files are sufficient for MVP.

## Can we fetch additional resources from NISR?
Yes, and you must log provenance (source URL, access time, status, notes).

## Are PDFs in scope?
Yes. Reports/questionnaires are valid resources in this challenge.

## What if metadata is missing?
Show missingness and caveats explicitly in your app.

## What matters most in judging?
Clear user value, trustworthy outputs, runnable app, and policy relevance.
