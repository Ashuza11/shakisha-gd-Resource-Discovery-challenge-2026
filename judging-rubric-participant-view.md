# Judging Rubric (Participant View)

## Weights

- Coverage: 30%
- Usability: 25%
- Trustworthiness: 20%
- Maintainability: 15%
- Policy relevance: 10%

## What judges will verify

1. Streamlit app launches and runs
2. Discovery/dashboard flow works
3. Metadata and source access path are clear
4. Quality caveats are visible
5. At least one advocacy use case is demonstrated
6. Code and setup are maintainable

## How to maximize score

- Coverage: show meaningful breadth of resources and filters
- Usability: keep interaction simple and clear
- Trustworthiness: show citations, quality flags, and access status
- Maintainability: clean repo structure + clear setup instructions
- Policy relevance: end with a concrete decision-support example
