# Data Provenance Policy

For any resource validated or added during the hackathon, record:

1. Source institution
2. Source URL
3. Access timestamp
4. Access status (`available`, `restricted`, `not_found`, `broken_link`)
5. Notes on constraints or caveats

## NISR retrieval guidance

- Baseline CSVs in this repo are the starting inventory.
- Teams can validate key links directly from NISR during event time.
- Do not bypass restricted systems or private endpoints.

## Citation format

`Source: <Institution>, <Study Title>, <Year>, <URL>`
